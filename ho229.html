My First Aerospace Project

Built at age 13 with foam core construction, this prototype taught critical lessons in stability and propulsion.

Key Challenges

  • Propulsion Limitations: Improvised EDF design with thrust issues
  • Aerodynamic Instability: Bulkiness and structural weaknesses
  • Systems Integration: Early lessons in weight distribution

Legacy

Directly influenced later UAV designs like R.A.3.D. through iterative testing.
